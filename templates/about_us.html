About Us

Food is meant to be shared,and together

we can nourish our community with love and kindness.

FoodShare Hub was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to address the pressing issue of food waste and food insecurity simultaneously. We believe that every individual and business has a role to play in reducing food waste and ensuring that nutritious food reaches those in need. Driven by this vision, our team of dedicated professionals came together to develop innovative solutions that make it easy for individuals, businesses, and organizations to participate in food waste reduction and food sharing initiatives.

At Food ResQ, we believe that everyone has a role to play in creating a more sustainable and equitable food system. Whether you're a business looking to reduce food waste or an individual passionate about food sharing, we invite you to join us on this journey towards a brighter, more sustainable future.
